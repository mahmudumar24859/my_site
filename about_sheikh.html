Brief History of Sheikh Abdulwahab Abdallah

Sheikh Abdulwahab Abdallah

Imam Ahlussunnah Wal-Jama'a
Chairman Bn Baaz Islamic Foundation
sheikhabdulwahababdallah@gmail.com
WhatsApp: +2347018364232

Shaikh Abdulwahab is a renowned Islamic Scholar and preacher who has spent major part of his life in teaching and preaching of Islam. Together with Late Shaikh Ja’afar Mahmud Adam, they have contributed immensely in creating Islamic awareness within and outside the country. He is a graduate of Darul Hadith in Makkah and Islamic University of Madinah, Kulliyatul Hadith Wa Darasat al Islamiyya (Faculty of Hadith and Islamic Knowledge). He is the Chairman of Bin Baz Foundation, member, Shari’ah Commission of Zamfara State and member, National Supreme Council for Islamic Affairs (NSCIA). As part of his efforts towards propagation and development of Islam, Shaikh Abdulwahab has written several books on various topics including but not limited to Fatwa on Marriage and Divorce, Fatwa on Bid’a (innovation), issues of Hisba, Fatwa on Hajj (pilgrimage), Fatwa on Fasting among others. Some of his activities include weekly preaching at Usman Bin Affan Mosque Gadon Kaya, Lesson on the book of Sahih Muslim every weekend at Markaz Khulafa'ur rashidun, Sharada Federal Housing Estate, Weekly preaching for women and Sahihul Bukhari at Sharada, Fatwa with Radio Kano, Radio Express, Arewa Radio and a weekly program with the popular Africa TV3 and Sunnah TV. The Sheikh is married with children. May Almighty Allah Countinue to spare his life with Barakah and grant him Aljannatul Firdaus as his final Abode, Amin.
